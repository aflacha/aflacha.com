01 — About

More than a decade shaping digital experiences.

Started as a web front-end developer in 2013, moved through project management and freelance, then found my true calling in game design in late 2015. Today I'm COO & Co-founder of Artelysium Studios.

I've worn many hats — Lead Game Designer, UX/UI Designer, Producer, Product Lead. What ties it all together? I build things that work, lead teams that trust each other, and ship without the drama.

I also founded Kolektif untuk Gamedev Indonesia, a community for Indonesian game developers. Outside the studio? I write on Medium.
